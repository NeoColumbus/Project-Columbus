# Dead Wall / Beige Building Report Card

<!-- HUMAN NOTE: This is not anti-housing. It is pro-housing with public beauty and street life. -->

## Line

Density deserves beauty.

## Report

Place:

Street:

What fails:

- blank wall
- hostile ground floor
- no shade
- dead corner
- cheap materials
- no doors
- no benches
- no trees
- no night presence
- fake luxury beige

What works:

- active entrance
- windows
- local business
- shade
- durable materials
- stoop, porch, bench, or civic edge
- good lighting
- public art that is not filler

## One Sentence Demand

`More homes here, but make the street alive: [DEMAND].`

## Poster Lines

`Report a dead wall.`

`Build the city. Do not beige it to death.`

`More homes. More beauty. More life on the street.`
