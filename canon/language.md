# Language

Full City Columbus speaks in public rhythm.

Short lines.
Hard nouns.
Repetition with purpose.
No fog.

The voice is severe, beautiful, civic, loved, fanatical, sincere, anti-beige, grounded in Columbus, and grand without becoming fake.

## Core Lines

No more half-city.

No more beige life.

The body exists. Write the word.

Columbus is not becoming real. It is becoming legible.

Columbus was always here. Now it arrives.

Neo Columbus arrives.

The first geometry was earth. The second geometry is city.

The city was beneath us the whole time.

COTA is not charity. It is the city's nervous system.

The capital should look like a capital.

Build the city. Do not beige it to death.

The machine can help us draft. It cannot love Columbus for us.

## Sentence Law

If a sentence sounds like it came from a chamber luncheon, delete it.

If a line can fit on a flyer and survive being shouted under an overpass, keep it.

If the language loses the city, cut the language.
