# Symbols

<!-- HUMAN NOTE: Symbols must avoid costume history and fake ancient styling. Use geometry as obligation, not wallpaper. -->

## Primary Motifs

- Circle: gathering, center, measure, public form.
- Axis: Broad and High, routes, state power, civic decision.
- Confluence: Scioto and Olentangy, meeting, crossing, pressure.
- Line: transit, desire path, nervous system.
- Stone: Statehouse severity, courthouse mass, public memory.
- Brick: neighborhoods, alleys, labor, weather.
- Amber signal: bus, rail, crossing light, city pulse.

## Never Use

- Faux-ancient pattern packs.
- Costume props.
- Sacred forms treated as wallpaper.
- Columbus explorer imagery.
- Generic skyline clip art.
- Flags that look purchased from a mall brand.

## Working Mark Idea

A circle crossed by two civic axes.

One axis is river.
One axis is street.

The mark should feel older than branding and newer than a zoning map.
