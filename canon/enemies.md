# Enemies

This project is loved, not hateful.

But love needs enemies.

## Named Enemies

- Beige life.
- Half-city thinking.
- Civic embarrassment.
- OSU-only identity.
- Parking-lot mentality.
- Consultant language.
- Generic development.
- Transit shame.
- Flags that look like Banana Republic knockoffs.
- Cities that grow without becoming legible.
- Bureaucracy that sands beauty into oatmeal.

## The Point

The enemy is not a neighbor.
The enemy is not a rider.
The enemy is not a renter.
The enemy is not a suburb kid who wants something to belong to.

The enemy is the force that teaches a city to expect less of itself.
