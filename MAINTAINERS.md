# Maintainers

Full City Columbus is in early founder-led mode.

That means the project has a signal and a repo, but the maintainer table is still forming.

## Current Status

Project status:
v0.2 scaffold, public site, Drop 001, street kit, policy stack, research spine.

Maintainer model:
founder-led until repeat contributors earn trust and take ownership of lanes.

## Needed Hands

Myth and language:
keeps the public voice sharp.

Research:
checks history, sources, dates, and local claims.

Design:
keeps the visual system hard, legible, printable, and alive.

Street kit:
turns drops into posters, stickers, handbills, and field objects.

Site and code:
keeps the public page fast, readable, accessible, and easy to publish.

Policy:
turns the myth into buildable civic demands.

Community:
helps issues, first contributions, field reports, and proof wall entries move.

## Maintainer Standard

A maintainer should protect both force and accuracy.

No fake authority.
No generic filler.
No unsourced certainty.
No design that collapses when it hits the street.

The project should become more open without becoming weaker.
