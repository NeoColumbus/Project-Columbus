Full City Columbus treats policy as public form.

Housing is form.
Transit is form.
Safety is form.
Street trees are form.
Zoning is form.
The bus stop is form.

Do not write policy notes that could belong anywhere.
Make them answer Columbus.

## Policy Law

No policy without beauty.
No beauty without material life.
