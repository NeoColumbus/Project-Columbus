## Position

A city becomes safer when daily life becomes less abandoned.

That means:

- slower deadly streets
- safer pedestrian crossings
- fewer vacant dead zones
- more stable housing
- public bathrooms where needed
- clean, lit, cared-for transit stops
- youth spaces that are not afterthoughts
- crisis response that does not turn every crisis into a weapon
- design that supports presence without turning the city into a cage

## Language Rule

Do not use safety as a mask for making poor people disappear.
Do not use compassion as a mask for ignoring harm.

Tell the truth both ways.
