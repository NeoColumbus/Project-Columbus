COTA is not charity.

It is the city's nervous system.

## Position

Transit should be treated as public structure.

That means:

- more frequent service
- better shelters
- safer crossings to stops
- clear route information
- reliable night and weekend service
- real bus priority where streets choke service
- connections between neighborhoods, not only lines pointed downtown
- dignity for disabled riders
- dignity for workers whose shifts do not fit office hours

## Public Form

A bus stop is not a pole in the weather.

It is a room in the city.

If the room has no roof, no bench, no light, no trash can, no safe crossing, and no clear sign, the city is saying something.

Say something better.
