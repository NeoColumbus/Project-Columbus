# Code Of Conduct

<!-- HUMAN NOTE: Keep the room buildable. This is not a corporate values poster. It is a guardrail for public work. -->

Full City Columbus is open to people who want to make the city stronger, truer, more beautiful, and more usable.

Bring heat to the work.
Do not bring poison to the room.

## Expected

- Argue with ideas and artifacts.
- Correct mistakes directly.
- Cite factual claims.
- Credit borrowed work.
- Disclose AI help when it matters.
- Leave room for novices.
- Make the project more real.

## Not Allowed

- threats or harassment
- doxxing or private-information exposure
- slurs aimed at contributors
- spam, bot accounts, or fake local support
- fake official affiliation
- stolen assets
- intentional misinformation
- intimidation around identity, neighborhood, class, religion, disability, age, sex, or race

## Enforcement

Maintainers can remove comments, close issues, reject pull requests, block accounts, or limit participation when someone makes the project unsafe, fake, or unusable.

If something needs attention, open an issue or contact a maintainer.

The goal is simple:

keep the signal public,
keep the work honest,
keep the room buildable.
