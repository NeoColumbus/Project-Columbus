# Manifesto

<!-- HUMAN NOTE: This is a first manifesto draft, not scripture. Keep the force. Correct the facts. Remove false grandeur. -->

Columbus does not suck.

Columbus is unfinished.

It has the body of a city.

Rivers.
Roads.
Hospitals.
Universities.
Immigrants.
Suburbs.
Towers.
Apartments.
Neighborhoods.
Logistics.
Money.
Highways.
State power.
Scale.

What it lacks is the animating word.

The city is not dead.
The city is unnamed inside its own name.

## The Arrival

Columbus is the legal name.
Neo Columbus is the voltage inside it.

Do not ask permission from the old seal.
Do not wait for a committee to bless the signal.

Make the city so legible the name catches up.

## The Older City

The legal city begins in 1812.

But the center is older than the charter.
The rivers are older than the map.
The crossing is older than the seal.
The geometry is older than the grid.

Before Columbus had a name, Ohio already knew monumental public form.

The first geometry was earth.
The second geometry is city.

## The Half-City

Columbus has learned to describe itself in fragments.

The campus.
The suburb.
The district.
The event.
The restaurant street.
The downtown shot from the river.

Fragments are not enough.

An event is not a city.
A district is not a city.
A university is not a city.
A downtown is not a city.
A city is when the whole body starts to move.

No more half-city.

## The Nervous System

COTA is not charity.

It is not a favor.
It is not a last resort.
It is not background equipment for people with fewer options.

COTA is the city's nervous system.

Transit is how the city recognizes itself across distance.
Transit is how a body knows it has limbs.
Transit is how a capital stops pretending that every citizen arrives by private capsule.

## The Anti-Beige Law

Ugliness is not neutral.

Cheap blankness is not humility.
Beige development is not innocence.
The city does not owe its future to value-engineered despair.

Build housing.
Build transit.
Build density.
Build safety.
Build beautiful ordinary life.

Build the city.
Do not beige it to death.

## Neo Columbus

Neo Columbus is not a fantasy map.
It is not a mascot.
It is not a brand mood.

Neo Columbus is the awakened form of the city already here.

The future city arrives through the old name and changes what it means.

Neo Columbus arrives from the future to call the hidden city forward.

Columbus was always here.
Now it arrives.

## Founding Law

Better to fail as myth than succeed as oatmeal.
