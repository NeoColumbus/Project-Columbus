# Marketing

<!-- HUMAN NOTE: Marketing here means public signal, not corporate persuasion. Make people want the city. Keep the work beautiful, repeatable, and stealable. -->

Full City Columbus marketing should feel like Earthwork Futurism:

old ground,
new machine,
public myth you can wear.

The goal is not to explain the whole project every time.

The goal is to make the signal irresistible enough that people ask what it means.

## Marketing Law

Signal first.
Line second.
Demand third.
Source trail fourth.

If the first move is a paragraph, the poster already lost.

## Drop 001 Law

Post the signal.
Name the break.
Build the body.

That is the entry point.

The campaign should not ask people to understand the whole theory before they can participate.

## What This Should Feel Like

Official enough to look inevitable.

Street enough to feel alive.

Simple enough to steal.

Strange enough to remember.

Local enough that it could not belong anywhere else.
