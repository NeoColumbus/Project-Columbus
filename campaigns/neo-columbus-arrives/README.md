## Key Line

Columbus was always here. Now it arrives.

## The Claim

The future city arrives through the grid, the river, the bus line, the arena noise, the winter street, the tower light, the block party, the hospital shift, the basement show, the Statehouse stone.

Old Columbus is municipal sleep, OSU plus suburbs, parking-lot capital, beige growth.

Neo Columbus is the second geometry.
The city beneath the city.
The word arriving in the body.
