# Viral And Guerrilla Marketing Notes

<!-- HUMAN NOTE: Use this as campaign physics, not as a copybook. Do not clone protected logos, mascots, names, or campaign art. Extract mechanics. Make them Columbus. -->

The poster tags felt half done because they were mostly lines.

The strongest public campaigns create behavior:

- repeat a symbol
- create a ritual
- leave evidence
- invite personalization
- give people a role
- make the share object beautiful enough to travel

## Case Lessons

## Obey / Andre The Giant

Mechanic:
one repeated icon becomes a public question.

Useful lesson:
Do not over-explain the signal. Make it show up until people ask what it means.

Source:
https://obeygiant.com/propaganda/manifesto/

## The Blair Witch Project

Mechanic:
the campaign treated fiction like found evidence: missing posters, documents, clips, and rumor.

Useful lesson:
Make campaign artifacts feel like field evidence, not ads.

Source:
https://www.latimes.com/entertainment-arts/movies/story/2024-07-30/the-blair-witch-project-marketing-25th-anniversary-1999-project

## ALS Ice Bucket Challenge

Mechanic:
a simple participatory ritual made the cause legible and repeatable.

Useful lesson:
Give people one action they can perform, film, nominate, repeat, and localize.

Source:
https://www.als.org/ibc

## Red Bull Stratos

Mechanic:
turn the brand into a live mission with a countdown, stakes, and public witness.

Useful lesson:
Drops should feel like missions, not content calendars.

Source:
https://blog.youtube/news-and-events/mission-complete-red-bull-stratos-lands/

## Share A Coke

Mechanic:
personalization turned a product into an identity object.

Useful lesson:
Let people put their place into the campaign: neighborhood, corridor, bus stop, block, building, river, route.

Source:
https://www.coca-colacompany.com/about-us/history/how-a-campaign-got-its-start-down-under

## Spotify Wrapped

Mechanic:
personal data became a shareable identity card.

Useful lesson:
Make city audits shareable: bus stop score, dead wall report, neighborhood line, AI dividend test.

Source:
https://newsroom.spotify.com/2025-12-03/wrapped-marketing-campaign/

## Barbie Movie Rollout

Mechanic:
the campaign made the world recognizably itself through color, scale, merch, places, and repeated visual cues.

Useful lesson:
Earthwork Futurism needs world takeover discipline: flag, sticker, poster, avatar, handbill, street action, social frame.

Source:
https://shortyawards.com/16th/barbie-the-movie-marketing-campaign

## Rules For Full City Columbus

1. Symbol first.
2. Evidence object second.
3. Public ritual third.
4. Personalization fourth.
5. Civic demand fifth.
6. Source trail sixth.
