## Working Frame

COTA is not charity.
It is the city's nervous system.

## Current Research Hook

COTA identifies itself as the regional public transit provider for greater Columbus and Central Ohio. Its public materials list fixed-route bus service, COTA Mainstream paratransit, COTA//Plus on-demand microtransit, and LinkUS modernization work.
