# Source Sprints

<!-- HUMAN NOTE: This is the bridge from heat to receipts. Public copy can stay mythic. Claims that become testimony, policy, or recruiting material need sources. -->

Run one sprint at a time.

Each sprint should produce:

- one corrected research file
- one short source note
- one public line that survives correction
- one list of what still needs local eyes

## Sprint 001: Growth, Annexation, And Fragments

Question:

Is Columbus experienced as one body or as annexed fragments stitched around corridors, suburbs, campuses, highways, and downtown pieces?

Start with:

- [City of Columbus 2026 Community Profile PDF](https://www.columbus.gov/files/sharedassets/city/v/1/finance/budget/2026/008-2026.pdf)
- [City of Columbus historical milestones](https://www.columbus.gov/Services/Columbus-Water-Power/About-Columbus-Water-Power/Our-History/Water-Treatment-History/Historical-Milestones-for-Wastewater-Treatment-in-Columbus)

Needs:

- annexation timeline
- municipal boundary maps
- population and job geography
- neighborhood examples
- where the city feels whole
- where the city breaks

Output file:

- [columbus-history.md](columbus-history.md)

## Sprint 005: Earthworks And Civic Form
