## The Deep Place

UNESCO describes the Hopewell Ceremonial Earthworks as eight monumental earthen enclosure complexes built roughly 2,000 to 1,600 years ago along central tributaries of the Ohio River. UNESCO emphasizes scale, precise geometry, vast level plazas, solar and lunar alignments, and long-distance exchange through materials brought from far outside the immediate region.

The National Park Service notes that the Hopewell Ceremonial Earthworks became the United States' 25th UNESCO World Heritage site on September 19, 2023. NPS describes enormous squares, circles, octagons, hilltop forms, lunar and solar alignments, and a standard unit of measure repeated across the works.

Ohio History Connection calls the Newark Earthworks the largest set of geometric earthen enclosures in the world. It notes that the surviving Great Circle, Octagon, and Wright Square are remnants of a once roughly four-square-mile complex.

### Megalopolis And The Great City

Megalopolis means Great City.

Pausanias says the Arcadians united into Megalopolis to gain strength. Visit Peloponnese dates the city's founding to 371 BC and describes it as a new major urban center formed by gathering surrounding settlements, with a theater that seated around 20,000 people.

This matters because "metropolis" is not just a census category. Merriam-Webster traces metropolis through Greek roots meaning mother plus city, while modern English uses it for a chief or large center.

The word-family gives the project a spine:

Not a suburb pile.

Many settlements.

## Campbell Myth Structure

The Joseph Campbell Foundation summarizes Campbell's four functions of living mythology:

- mystical: awe before mystery
- cosmological: an image of the universe
- sociological: a society's order and norms
- psychological or pedagogical: guidance through life stages

The order matters for this project.

Do not start with the sociological function.
That is where policy lives.
That is where everything becomes a meeting.

Start with awe.
Start with cosmos.
Start with age.
Start with the body.

Then the public program has somewhere to stand.
